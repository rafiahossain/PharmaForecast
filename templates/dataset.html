Datasets

1. Consumption Report Item Wise

Period: 01/01/2024 – 31/05/2025

Purpose: Time Series Analysis of Drug Consumption

  • Key Characteristics:
    • Item-wise drug usage with timestamps
    • Fields: Date, Drug Code/Name, Quantity, Unit
    • Daily or monthly granularity
  • Use in Time Series Analysis:
    • Forecast drug demand (ARIMA, Prophet, LSTM)
    • Identify trends, seasonal spikes, and anomalies
    • Improve inventory management and reduce stockouts

2. GRN Register (Supplier Wise)

Period: 01/01/2024 – 31/05/2025

Purpose: Supply Chain Risk Management (SCRM)

  • Key Characteristics:
    • Logs of goods received from suppliers
    • Fields: GRN Date, Supplier, Item, Quantity, Delay, PO
    • Supplier-level delivery performance
  • Use in SCRM:
    • Track supplier delays or incomplete deliveries
    • Correlate supply with consumption patterns
    • Identify supply risks or bottlenecks
