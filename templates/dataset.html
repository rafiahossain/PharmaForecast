1. Consumption Report Item Wise

Period: Jan 2024 – May 2025

Purpose: Regression Analysis of Item Consumption

  • Key Characteristics:
    • Item-wise drug and equipment usage per month and stock data
    • Essential Fields: ItemName, Unit Name, Current Stock, Jan 2024, Feb 2024 ... May 2025
    • Monthly granularity. There should be atleast 12 months of consumption columns in the raw data.
  • Use in Regression for Demand Forecasting:
    • Forecast seasonal demand (Ridge, Lasso, ANN, etc.)
    • Identify trends, seasonal spikes, and anomalies
    • Improve inventory management

2. GRN Register (Supplier Wise)

Period: Jan 2024 – May 2025

Purpose: Supply Chain Risk Management (SCRM)

  • Key Characteristics:
    • Logs of goods received from suppliers
    • Essential Fields: Supplier Name, Grn No, Grn Date, Payment Status, Challan No./ Date, PO No. / Date, Bill No. / Date, Category Name, Sub Category, Item Name, Batch No, Expiry Data, Rec. Qty., PO Unit Rate, Unit Cost, MRP, Item Value.
    • Supplier risk, expiry and delivery performance
  • Use in SCRM:
    • Track supplier delays or incomplete deliveries
    • Identify near-expiry items
    • Identify supply risks or bottlenecks
